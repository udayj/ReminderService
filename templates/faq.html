General Questions

1. How does it work ?

You simply need to instruct the system what to do (email/sms/phone), when to do and how frequently to do it (once/daily/weekly/monthly). The service then makes sure that your task is done.

2. How do I know if the task was done successfully ?

As soon as the task is done, the system gathers the status information and makes it available on the task details page. This status information is generally available within 5 minutes after the task is performed.
