What does it do ?

Our state of the art technology helps you automate the mundane but important tasks in your business. You can use our simple and cost effective reminder service to remember important issues of your business. People routinely use our service for tasks such as reminding their clients of payment due dates, setting appointment reminders, reminding themselves of tax filing due dates, reminding themselves of an important upcoming meeting, reminding customers of service due dates. Some companies also use the service to streamline their internal operations.
Reminders can be sent on email, SMS or through a voice phone call - depending on your choice.
